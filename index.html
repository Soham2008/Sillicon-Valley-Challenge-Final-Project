Calculation of the foundation plan

The foundation plan is a plan view drawing, in section, showing the location and size of footings, piers, columns, foundation walls, and supporting beams

These are some important points to be remembered for a perfect foundation plan:-

  • Plot area:- The area which is surrounded by a boundary line (fencing) is called as Plot Area
  • Concrete Volume:- Concrete Volume is an amount of space that occupied by concrete.
  • Perimeter plate:- Perimeter plate refers to the measurement of the boundary that surrounds the plot
  • Lateral surface area:- Lateral surface area refers to how much area the surface of the plot take up all together.

These are the some of the most important formulae needed to be remembered for the dream house

  • Centimetres to Inches:- To convert centimetres to inches, multiply the given centimetres value by 0.393701.
  • Inches to Centimetres:- To convert inches to centimetres, multiply the given inches value by 2.54.
  • Metres to feet:- To convert metres to feet, multiply the given metres value by 3.28084.
  • Feet to Metres:- To convert feet to metres, multiply the given feet value by 3.28084.
  • Metres to Yards:- To convert metres to yards, multiply the given metres value by 1.093613.
  • Yards to Metres:- To convert yards to metres, multiply the given yards value by 0.9144.
